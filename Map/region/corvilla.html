Corvilla Island

Corvilla Island is a small, remote location off the main continent and difficult to reach. Not much is known about it, the closest region to it is Rannikkyo far to the east

Cities:
The only city on the island is Corvilla, hence the name of the island. Not much is known about it

Landmark:
Corvilla has a tall mountain on it, although not much is known otherwise.

Environment:
Not much is known of the environment of Corvilla Island, only knowledge is of how rough and deadly the ocean surrounding the island is.

Wildlife and Monsters:
No information.
