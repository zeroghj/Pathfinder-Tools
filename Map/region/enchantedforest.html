Enchanted Forest

Very little is known about the Enchanted Forest, it is a land ruled by mystical creatures, you can encounter marvelous things in this region that can struck awe or fear. Fey are rumored to exists and the Gnome City is located in this region too.

Cities:
The Gnome City is located in the mountain to the south-west and it is rumored that a city of fey exist within the forest, but no one can claim to have seen it.

Landmark:
It is rumored that within the enchanted forest lies a very special world called the Crystal Forest. The Forest has trees made out of magic crystals and close to it there is a cave where extremely valuable magical crystal can be harvested, or so people like to say when telling drunk stories at the local inn.

Environment:
The Enchanted Forest get its name from the earie magical energy that emits from it. Not many people dare venture into it, weird things tends to happen to those who wander within it and there are strong magic at plays that deter weak willed individuals to wander within. The Enchanted Forest has a vast network of lake and rivers that stem from the Golden Mountains to the West.

Wildlife and Monsters:
The wildlife in the enchanted forest is unlike anywhere else on the continent, the magical energy attract weird magical being that can overthrow unprepare adventurers really easily. Weird things are roaming those woods, magical being, ethereal being, not the kind of creature you want to take lightly.
