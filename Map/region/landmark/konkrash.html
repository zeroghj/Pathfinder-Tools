Stronghold Konkrash

Konkrash is the biggest Orc setlement on the continent and it has existed before human presence. It rules the southern portion of the Eulyssian region and bring fear and terror to the Kingdom of Eulice. It has been on a never ending war with the Kingdom of Eulice and has never had any major defeats, it is not known why the Orc of Konkrash have yet to give a greater assault on the Kingdom of Eulice, but no one dares even ask the question.
