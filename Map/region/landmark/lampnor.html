City of Lampnor

The City of Lampnor is well known accross the world, its great lighthouse is a sign of relief on the face of every navigators that comes into the region, a sign that they made it all the way alive. While the city is not directly accessible from the Ocean, it has become a large-ish city throughout the years as a place where navigators from all around the planet comes to visit and stay before leaving once again. There are not many inter-continental voyage and very little is known of the other continents, but those brave enough to do the journey are often found in this town.
