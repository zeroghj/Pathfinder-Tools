Lashandia

Lashandia is another city part of the Elven Nation of Rannikkyo, it is quite populous but much less visually impressive than Sunstride.

The city is more accepting of adventurers, but they remain skeptical of foreigners. The city does not have as much magic as Sunstride, instead most of the city focus on physical work, nature and a strong military organization to fend off any attack from Trolls.
