Twirivea

Twirivea is a rich town filled with noble and rich merchant. It is located near a peaceful river and stand between Inzan and Fandor. This prime located coupled with the beautiful view around the town has made of the location one of high value estate. There is a lot of guards and military presence in the town and commoners are not welcomed there. It is not a place for adventurer to wander to, they are not welcomed.
