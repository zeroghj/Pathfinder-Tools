Zendan

Zendan is a dwarven city that lies on the southern end of the Tontarrian Mountains. The city is the last mining city of the dwarven kingdom. The other cities have been mined out long ago and Zendan now is the last city to actively manufacture and gather minerals from the mountains. Because of this sector of activity, Zendan is by far the dirtiest of the dwarven fortress. The dwarves there are rougher, the bar brawls more common and they are less welcoming of strangers.
