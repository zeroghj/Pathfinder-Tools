Northern Fandor

Northern Fandor is part of the Kingdom of Fandor, it is the region in which the capital of the kingdom is located alongside most of its population. Northern Fandor is a vast fertile region which is filled with farmlands and prosperous cities.

Cities:
Most cities within Northern Fandor are part of the Kingdom of Fandor and are primarily human in ethnicity. Fandor is the capital of the Kingdom and alongside with Seranya, they are the two largest city in the region and they reside around Lake Tsundalis along with the village of Sirl. Sirl is a small residential village where very little action occurs.

Northern Fandor also has the village of Etroin located on a narrow gap of the Feldin river, continuing along the river, right before it meets the Ocean, you will find the City of Lampnor, the town is most known for its huge Lighthouse. On an Island not far from there you can find the isolated fishing town of Intla.
Northern Fandor has numerous smaller town, The biggest of which is Ponster from the North Side Farmlands and Harnos from the East Side Farmlands. Those villages are only notable because they are about the only place in the farmlands where you can get adventurer equipment.

While everything named so far had been human settlement part of the Kingdom of Fandor, there is one major Orc Stronghold that has sprung up in recent years in Northern Fandor. Stronghold Bartark is a major new player in the region, they are an aggressive Orc tribe that has been pillaging the nearby lands and given the human Kingdom a lot of trouble by their repeated aggression and act of war.
Landmark:
Most of the major landmark of Northern Fandor are its two main farmlands, the North Side Farmlands and the East Side Farmlands, they are very similar in composition and role, they are simply divided because of their geographical location. One Major Landmark is the Grand Battlefield, it is currently a scorched by war land where the Kingdom of Fandor and the Stronghold of Bartark fight each other daily for control over the region. It is said that thousands of bodies lies in the field and gets added daily.

Environment:
Northern Fandor is a very flat land with the Feldin river as its most distinguishable feature. It runs through it from Central Fandor to the Northern Ocean. Northern Fandor access to the Ocean is limited, most of the coast is 100 meter high cliffs with very little access to the water. The northern region of Northern Fandor is made of highlands and there are some wildlife and such wandering the land. The central portion of the land is very civilized, farmland and cities occupies most of this portion of the region.

Wildlife and Monsters:
Most of the wildlife of Northern Fandor lives in the Highlands in the north of the region. The rest of the region is populated by humans or orc.
